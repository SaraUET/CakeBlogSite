Coconut Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 box white cake mix (any brand)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon coconut extract
  • 1 medium can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 small can of cream of coconut
  • 1 container of frozen whipped topping
  • 1½ cups shredded coconut

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Grease a 9-inch pan with oil and pour the batter into it.
  4. Bake the cake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. When the cake is baked, take it out of the oven and allow it to cool down, then remove it from the pan.

For topping:

  1. Once the cake is cool, spread the whipped topping over the top of the cake.
  2. Sprinkle the shredded coconut over the whipped topping evenly.
  3. Chill the cake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
