Carrot Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, sugars, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. Add oil, eggs, grated carrots, nuts, pineapple, and vanilla. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased and floured cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool completely before frosting.
