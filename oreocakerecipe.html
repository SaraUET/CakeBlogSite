Oreo Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 cups crushed Oreo cookies
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 package chocolate cake mix
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup crushed Oreo cookies (for filling)
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • Crushed Oreo cookies (for garnish, if desired)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, combine the crushed Oreo cookies and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared cake pans.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chocolate cake mix, water, vegetable oil, and eggs. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter evenly over the Oreo crust in the cake pans.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cakes to cool completely in the pans.
  7. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form.
  8. Carefully remove one cake layer from the pan and place it on a serving plate.
  9. Spread crushed Oreo cookies over the cake layer, then top with a layer of whipped cream.
  10. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining whipped cream.
  11. Garnish with crushed Oreo cookies before serving.
