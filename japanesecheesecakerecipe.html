Japanese Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 6 large eggs, separate the yolk from the whites
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease and line the bottom of a 9-inch round cake pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter over low heat. Add cream cheese and sugar, and stir until smooth. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  3. In a bowl, beat egg yolks and the cooled butter and cream cheese mixture, vanilla extract, milk, flour, cornstarch, and salt until smooth.
  4. In another bowl, beat egg whites and cream of tartar until stiff peaks form.
  5. Fold the egg whites into the batter gently until well combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  7. Place the cake pan in a larger dish and add hot water to the dish to create a water bath.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 60-70 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cake is set.
  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool completely in the pan.
  10. Once cooled, remove the cake from the pan and serve.
