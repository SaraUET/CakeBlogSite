Orange Cake

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour - 1 1/2 cups
  • Granulated sugar - 1 cup
  • Baking soda - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Large eggs (room temperature) - 2
  • Unsalted butter (melted) - 1/2 cup
  • Fresh orange juice - 1/3 cup
  • Orange zest - 1 tablespoon
  • Buttermilk (or simple milk) - 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon

Instructions for Cake:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Grease a 9-inch pan with oil and pour the batter into it.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Allow the cake to cool down, then remove it from the pan.

Orange Glaze (Optional):

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, orange juice, and orange zest until smooth.
  2. Add the glaze over the cooled cake.
